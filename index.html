SENSOR SUITE

Distributed Fusion Architecture

Nine custom AI vision modules power autonomous decision-making across every flight phase. The dual-Pi architecture distributes sensor processing — forward-facing systems on Pi #1, downward-facing on Pi #2 — for redundancy and specialized computation.
